Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our banking services

Account Management

How do I open a new account?

Opening an account is easy! Click on "Open Account" in the navigation menu, fill out the registration form with your personal details, and submit. Your account will be created instantly with a unique account number. You can start using your account immediately after verification.

What documents do I need to open an account?

For online account opening, you'll need your full legal name, email address, phone number, date of birth, and a valid residential address. For certain account types or higher limits, additional verification may be required including government-issued ID.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the sign-in page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a secure password reset link. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password. For security, the link expires after 24 hours.

Transfers & Payments

How long do transfers take?

Internal transfers between Heritage Bank accounts are instant. External transfers to other banks typically take 1-3 business days depending on the receiving bank. Wire transfers are usually completed within 24 hours for domestic and 2-5 business days for international.

What are the transfer limits?

Daily transfer limits vary by account type. Standard accounts have a $10,000 daily limit, while premium accounts have limits up to $50,000. You can view and request limit increases through your account settings or by contacting customer support.

Can I schedule recurring transfers?

Yes! You can set up recurring transfers for weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly schedules. Simply go to Transfers, select "Schedule Transfer," and choose your preferred frequency. You can manage or cancel scheduled transfers anytime from your dashboard.

Security

How is my account secured?

We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption, multi-factor authentication, and real-time fraud monitoring. All passwords are hashed using industry-standard bcrypt algorithm. We also offer optional two-factor authentication (2FA) for additional security.

What should I do if I notice suspicious activity?

Immediately contact our 24/7 fraud hotline at 1-800-HERITAGE. You can also temporarily freeze your account from the security settings in your dashboard. Report any unauthorized transactions within 60 days for full protection under our fraud guarantee.

Is my money FDIC insured?

Yes! Heritage Bank is a member of the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC). Your deposits are insured up to $250,000 per depositor, per account category. This means your money is protected even in the unlikely event of bank failure.

Loans & Credit

What types of loans do you offer?

We offer Personal Loans, Home Mortgages, Auto Loans, and Business Loans. Each loan type has competitive rates and flexible terms. Personal loans range from $1,000 to $50,000, while mortgages and business loans have higher limits based on qualification.

How do I apply for a loan?

Log into your account and navigate to "Request Loan." Fill out the application with your loan type, amount, purpose, and income information. Applications are typically reviewed within 24-48 hours, and you'll receive notification of the decision via email.

What affects my loan approval?

Loan approval depends on factors including credit history, income verification, debt-to-income ratio, and employment stability. Having an existing account with Heritage Bank in good standing can also positively impact your application.

Cards

How do virtual cards work?

Virtual cards are digital debit or credit cards that exist only in our app. They have a unique card number, expiry date, and CVV. Use them for online shopping, subscriptions, or anywhere digital payments are accepted. They're perfect for enhanced security since you can generate new numbers anytime.

Can I freeze my card temporarily?

Yes! You can instantly freeze and unfreeze your card from the Cards section in your dashboard. When frozen, all transactions will be declined until you unfreeze it. This is useful if you've misplaced your card or suspect unauthorized use.
